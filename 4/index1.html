Oman is a country located in the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, bordered by the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen. Despite being a small country, Oman has a rich history and culture that dates back to the ancient times. One of the fun facts about Oman is that it is the oldest independent state in the Arab world, with a history that spans over 5000 years. Oman is also known for its frankincense, which is one of the country's most valuable exports. If you're looking for a unique travel experience, Oman is definitely a destination worth considering. The country offers a mix of natural wonders, cultural attractions, and adventure activities that cater to all types of travelers.
